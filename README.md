# PaperCloak

Want to sneak some reading without getting caught? PaperCloak transforms any PDF into a legit-looking scientific paper—with fancy sections, fake abstracts, and APA-style references. Perfect for hiding your reading in plain sight at work or just enjoying your PDFs with a scholarly twist.

## Features

- Choose one-column thesis style or two-column journal layout.
- Auto-generate believable abstracts and fake references.
- Download or copy your disguised paper with ease.

## How to Use

Upload your PDF, tweak the title/authors/layout if you want, hit “Transform,” and boom! Your undercover paper is ready.

## Deployment & Development

Runs entirely in your browser — no backend needed. Host on GitHub Pages or similar to share.
